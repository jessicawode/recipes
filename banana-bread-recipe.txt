Banana bread

1 cup sugar
½ cup canola oil
2 eggs, beaten
3 well-ripened bananas
¼ cup hot water
2 cups sifted flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
½ teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon cinnamon
½ teaspoon salt

1.	Cream oil and sugar.
2.	Mix with mashed bananas, then beaten eggs.
3.	Mix together dry ingredients. Add to above mixture, alternately with hot water.
4.	Pour in loaf pan, which has been lined with foil and waxed paper.
5.	Bake in slow oven 350-375 degrees until toothpick comes out clean (about 45 minutes).
